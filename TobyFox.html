    Before Prominence

    Toby Fox is a fairly new name to videogames. His first publically released and recognized project was the EarthBound Halloween Hack which was a fanmade hack of the Nintendo SNES game Earthbound that added some more horror-related themes. After that, he worked on the music for a fairly well-known webcomic, Homestuck.

    Undertale

    Eventually, in 2013, Toby fox released the demo of a game he was working on, Undertale. For Undertale, Toby Fox was both the head programmer and composer. Undertale went on to be a significant success in 2015 when it came out, selling over a million copies. Along with it, the music was also regarded well. In fact, it was recieved so well that in 2020, Toby Fox held a live concert purely of Undertale's music. It was also streamed on Youtube. Overall, it was successful enough to allow Fox to release a sequel.

    Deltarune

    Ater the success of Undertale, Toby Fox began the develpment and compsing for Deltarune, its sequel. Its first chapter released on November 1, 2018. Since then, due to the COVID-19 pandemic, Fox has only been able to release one more chapter, but has stated that he has composed several pieces for the game when the programming is able to continue. As of October 30th, 2023, no new news has been reported on the last three chapters.

    Pokemon

    In addition to composing for his own games, Toby Fox has composed for GameFreak on the last two individual entries of Pokemon: Sword and Shield and Scarlet and Violet. For these, Fox composed the "Battle Tower" theme for Sword and Shield and the field theme of Scarlet and Violet.
