    • Masato Nakamura

    Nakamura was born in Chofu, Japan. Unlike many of the other composers on this list, Nakamura was largely a part of separate band before becoming a video game composer and still is. Nakamura has been a part of Dreams Come True since its inception in 1988. In it, he is the bassist. Dreams Come True managed to sell over a million copies with their first album. Meanwhile, in 1990, Masato Nakamura also began scoring for the original Sonic the Hedgehog. Also, unlike most other composers on this like, while he may have been fairly influential in video game music, he only composed songs for two original Sonic games (the first two) and for the re-release Sonic the Hedgehog 1&2. Other than that, he hasn't actually made much music for videogames, deciding to focus on Dreams Come True. So while he was very influential for his music in the original Sonic games, he is more memorable for Dreams Come True.
